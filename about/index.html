About Me

My name is Andrea Marangoni and I am a software engineer, graduated in Computer Science at University of Padua, Italy. It is at University where started my deep love for C++ language and where I started developing different projects of different complexities.

After graduation I went to work in an amazing startup called Vimage. (Link to the website). Here, I started to improve my skill in a commercial environment, developing 2D/3D interactive games, touchless application, Graphical User Interfaces; I was providing assistance to customers as well.

In 2013 I decided to have an experience outside Italy and that's why I moved. Currently I am living in London.
